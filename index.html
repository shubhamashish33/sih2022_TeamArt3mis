THINK COLLEGE

Think College provides a freely available, simplified platform for students who are in distress due to insufficient knowledge about various college courses. It provides all the undergraduate, post-graduate, and doctorate courses in a single platform. It will work as an extremely useful guide for students of the various domain to search, explore and select the courses of their choice. This website is also built for general users who want to have a general understanding of all the courses offered in colleges and have an insight into college courses. This website displays colleges such as IITs, NITs, and other Tier-3 colleges across India. This website will help students and other people to browse through the colleges under AICTE. The names of colleges are mentioned serially with the names of the courses they offer. Search option is available along with the selection of entries to be displayed. This website will even be beneficial for general users too.
